Oxen Corporation Division 2


A remote military compound situated far out beyond any civilization within the Sevari Sands. Wire fences surround the perimeter of one of the largest facility's run by the Oxen Corporation. The circumference of this installation spans for miles, housing the most confidential of Military Intelligence as well as some of the Oxen Corporation best kept secrets. Division 2 as it is called, is used as the boot camp to train new cadets and house important files and documents.


Oxen Corporations Storage Facility


A large warehouse located a couple of miles away from Division 2. It is believed to be the home to Ox Corp's larger arsenal of weapons as well as some secret projects that they want kept out of the public eye. Only personal with the highest clearance may enter and it is as highly guarded as the Camp itself.


Oxen Corporation Ordnance Range


A designated area for the testing of explosive devices and munitions. It is fenced off with many warnings not to enter the area if sounds of gunfire and explosions aren't enough to deter trespassers.


Ruins of Aurulant


Once a thriving abode to the Reign family, it was abandoned and now all that remains are is an empty shell of a building that is slowly eroding. It is reported that ghosts dressed as soldiers haunt the ruins but most agree those people have had too much sun.


The Empty Expanse


A large plain of nothing but sand as far as the eye can see. There is very little flora and virtually no fauna to speak of. To enter unprepared is surely a death sentence.
