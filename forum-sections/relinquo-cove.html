The Pier


A popular attraction spot, the Pier is not only home to some of the luxury boats but also Art Gallery's, Museums and a boardwalk where local musicians perform daily. The many restaurants and eatery's make this area a perfect family spot.


The All Fun Funfair


Located right beside the beach is the popular tourist attraction, The All Fun Funfair. Its attractions include the Dizzying Heights Roller Coaster, the only wooden roller coaster of its kind in Otara Mortus, and all the usual family favorites. Come on down, grab a hot dog, and enjoy some thrills at The All Fun Funfair.

Cove Beach


A long strip of golden sand greets the Onda Ocean at Cove Beach. Sun worshippers and surfers folk to the beach all year round as well as scuba divers who wish to explore the caves that have been created in the cliffs. It is the perfect family day out with food vendors, stalls, and merchants dotted along the sand.


Cliffside Mall


Above the beach is the popular Cliffside Mall. With designer brands, an upscale restaurant with great views of the ocean, and free parking, it's a great place to spend the afternoon and your money.


The Otara Mortus Natural History Museum


Sometimes described as ‘a museum of a museum’, it houses a wide-ranging and comprehensive zoological collection of more than 10,000 exhibits. Visitors can explore mammals, birds, fish, and insects, as well as mammals from around the world. Admission is free. Highlights include imposing skeletons of Lycan specimens that have been found or donated to the Museum by the Oxen Corporation.


The Marina


A dock with moorings and supplies for yachts and small boats. Tourists can choose to stay in houseboats as an alternative to traditional accommodation.
