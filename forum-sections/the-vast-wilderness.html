Misty Forest


Scattered throughout the Sanctum are large areas of thick forest. Named after the damp hue that hangs along the ground almost like a heavy fog. Visibility is poor as the canopy blocks almost all of the natural sunlight, making it an ideal hunting ground for predators.


Hell's Cavern


A hollow formed in the base of the mountain, it's surprisingly warm from the heat of the volcano it is formed from. The Kario's pack has claimed this territory as their own and reside within the cavern.


Summit Road


All trekkers must start here. Whether you wish to reach Montem Village or scale the mountains, the trail start's here. The road leads to the springs, the falls and ends at Montem Village. After that you must find your own way up if you wish to reach the summit.


Mount Hellion


A dormant volcano, Mount Hellion hasn't erupted in over ten thousand years. Geologists show great intrigue in the volcano to this day. Mountain climbers who scale the volcano will be treated to a swim in the lake that has formed within the old crater.


Hell Springs


At the base of the mountain, pools of water are heated by the magma underground. The water maintains a stable temperature and those with ailments or those just looking to relax, partake in the springs natural healing properties.


Brimstone Brook


The source of his brook comes from the crater on top of the mountain. Once the lake is full it falls down the side of the mountain. Because of the flow it picks up sediments from the mountain that changes the color of the water giving it reddish hue, hence the name.
