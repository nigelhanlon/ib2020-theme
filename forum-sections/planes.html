Mayhem


A deep pit of shadows and fire. From the underworld comes nightmares, ghosts, and demons. In its most terrible precincts sinners pay—some say eternally— for the crimes or debts they have accumulated in life. Overseen by Destiny Death.


Shadow Realm


The Shadow Realm itself is infinite, having no actual boundaries in any direction. It exists in and of itself, but is also tied to the material world in a very real way. It has its own geography, though it demonstrates some familiar similarities to the geography of the material world in some areas. This similarity can be misleading and some travelers of the Shadow Realm have gotten lost.
